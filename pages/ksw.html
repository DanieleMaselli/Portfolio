Digital interactive tour guide for cultural institutions

Date: 2020-2021 Research project My Role: UI-UX Design, Concept Development Tools: Figma Company: 3pc Neue Kommunikation GmbH & Qurator.ai

The aim of this project was to create an immersive audiovisual prototype that would allow users to experience a historical place or event. The Klassisch Stiftung Weimar offers a selection of audio guides through the various historical sites. The audio guides were analyzed in order to sort the different subtopics to create an interactive storytelling. This work resulted in the design of a unique platform where the users can navigate through an interactive guide that is directly controlled by themselves. The new designed audio guide could give the user enough flexibility to explore topics in depth without scrolling back on a timeline.

Introduction page showing the basic UI Elements with audio settings.

As the users continue listening to the story, images and associated descriptions of the historical sites follow.

First virtual branching of the storytelling allowing the users to choose whether deepen a particular aspect of the story, such as in this particular case a statue, or continue with the main audio path.

Infographics such as pictograms, maps or plans complement the audio explanation.
