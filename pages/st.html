STADTRADELN

Date: 2021-2023 Tools: Sketch, InVision, Figma My role: Art Direction, Concept development, UI-UX Design Design Team: Franziska Raue, Amy Gross
Company: Scholz & Volkmer GmbH

Stadtradeln is a campaign of the Climate Alliance network. The goal is to cycle as many kilometres as possible as a group or individual within a given period of time in order to reduce individual and municipal CO₂ emissions. I have been working as an Art Director for the Stadtradeln project for 2 years. My main task has been to work closely with the product owner and developers to improve the user experience and user interface. Here are some of the features that I have worked on.

Web Design

Redesing of the main navigation

STADTRADELN Blog

I crafted a dedicated blog page for Stadtradeln, providing a space for cyclists to seamlessly exchange their routes. This platform is designed to foster community engagement and facilitate the sharing of valuable insights and experiences among cyclists participating in Stadtradeln.

STADTRADELN Poster generator

I designed a poster generator concept for the Stadtradeln competition, empowering participating municipalities to craft their unique posters while adhering to our design guidelines. This tool not only streamlines the poster creation process but also ensures consistency and a cohesive visual identity across diverse locales.

STADTRADELN App - Achievement System

Achievement system to enhance the user experience in the Stadtradeln app. For this feature we developed a new concept where cyclists are rewarded for three categories: Kilometres, Routes and Series.
